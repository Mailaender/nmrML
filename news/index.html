nmrML news

  • August 10, 2017 NMR Converter available for Workflow integration at https://portaldev.phenomenal-h2020.eu/app-library.

  • July 30, 2017 Submission of nmrML paper to encourage peer community feedback.

  • December 8, 2016 All nmrML code examples have been overhauled.

  • November 29, 2016 HMDB supports nmrML and its nmrAssign tool allows nmr spectral annotation of identified compounds.

  • November 11, 2016 Release of nmrML-1.0-RC1 for for public consultation and feedback.

  • June 22, 2016 Metabolights database supports nmrML as NMR raw data format.

  • April 9, 2015 More and more parsers, converters and spectral processing tools are available.

  • February 22, 2014 nmrCV successfully merged and updated to fulfil nmrML requirements.

  • November 25, 2013 An initial version of the nmrML standard is currently under review by the EU FP7 COSMOS project (WP2)

  • November 15, 2013 Website is live

  • October 30 to 4 September, 2013 Second Hackathon on NMR Controlled Vocabulary and nmrML format standard, location IPB-Halle
